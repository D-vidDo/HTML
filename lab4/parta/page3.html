Soft Skills

The 3 soft skills that I believe are my strengths would be my curiosity [1], my adaptability [1], and my active listening skills [2].

My curiosity is an asset because whenever I am interested in something that I’m not knowledgeable on, I like to research on it and learn things about it. I always strive to learn as much as possible on topics that I’m interested in.

I am quite skilled at adapting to unique situations and will often find ways to solve problems as they arise. I work well under pressure and can adapt to my situations very quickly and integrate in different work situations well.

My active listening skills are also very good as I am often aware of my immediate surroundings and will build strong relationships with my peers by listening to what they have to say and relating to the topics at hand.

The 3 soft skills that I feel are my weakest areas are my time management [3], confidence [1], and leadership [2].

My time management skills are not that good as I tend to get side-tracked a lot and procrastinate when given a timeline to do things.

Another skill that I am weak in is my confidence, sometimes I cannot work up the confidence to stand up and defend myself when I am accused for some wrongdoings.

My leadership skills need to be worked on as well as I grew up being a follower rather than a leader. I tend to enjoy following directions more than giving them.
